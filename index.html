WHAT IS BEST ALL ABOUT?

The Bhawanipur Education Society College is a home to the Bhawanipur Entrepreneurship and Startup Training (BEST). The collective dedicated to fostering entrepreneurship and creating business opportunities for aspiring innovators. The collective offers mentorship sessions, factory visits, and immersive exposure to real-world business environments.

By engaging with established business leaders who share their experiences and insights, students gain practical knowledge and develop the skills required to navigate entrepreneurial challenges. B.E.S.T. equips young minds with the confidence, resilience, and vision needed to transform ideas into successful ventures.

Where innovation meets opportunity. Where dreams become reality. Where entrepreneurs are born.

General Guidelines

Essential rules and regulations to ensure a fair, professional, and competitive environment for all participants.

Participation Requirements

Participants must be in formal business attire
A valid student ID card is required for entry
Each college may register only one team per event
Cross-college teams are not allowed

Technical Requirements

Participants must carry their own laptops and pen drives
Internet access will not be provided during the events
Each team must carry their own laptop and pen drive on all event days

Conduct & Ethics

Use of AI tools, plagiarism, or any unethical practices is strictly prohibited
Use or possession of weapons, gutka, pan masala, tobacco, alcohol, or prohibited substances is strictly forbidden
Obscenity, inappropriate language, or unruly behavior will lead to disqualification
Plagiarism will lead to immediate disqualification

Event Logistics

Late submissions will incur penalties
Late submission of PPTs/deliverables will affect team scores
A maximum of 13 teams per event will be allowed
On-the-spot entries (OTSE) are subject to availability of slots

Authority & Decisions

The judges' decisions will be final and binding
Decisions of judges and organizing committee are final and binding
Organizers reserve the right to modify the rules and regulations (if required)
Right to admission is reserved

Responsibilities

Participants must not damage college property; any damage must be compensated and may lead to cancellation of the college registration
Organizers are not responsible for loss, theft, or damage of personal belongings
No expenses will be reimbursed by the host college

Important Notice

All participants are expected to maintain the highest standards of professionalism and integrity throughout the event. Violation of any guideline may result in immediate disqualification.

Registration Guidelines

First-Come-First-Served

All participating colleges are required to register for events online through Google Form, which will be sent to the college's official email ID. Slots filled on a first-come-first-served basis.

Registration Desk Timing

Registration desk closes 30 minutes before the scheduled event time. Participants are required to report to the registration desk at least 2 hours prior to their event.

Withdrawal Policy

Withdrawals after registration will result in negative marking. Withdrawal from an event after registration on the day of the event will result in negative marking.

Event Restrictions

Events having restrictions on the maximum number of participants/teams shall be accepted on a first-come-first-served basis. Maximum of 13 teams per event will be allowed.
